COVID-19 testing and care

The COVID-19 pandemic created sweeping changes in our day-to-day lives. Main Line Health's commitment to patient, family and employee safety are essential priorities as we continue to provide exceptional care to the community.

Find information about safety protocols in place across Main Line Health, COVID-19 testing, surgical and medical appointments and more in our COVID-19 Frequently Asked Questions page.

Visitation guidelines

The health and safety of our patients, families and staff is our top priority. Our visitation guidelines balance preventing the spread of COVID-19 with the needs of our patients and their loved ones. We continue to monitor the spread of COVID-19 in our community, and may update the guidelines as needed to provide safe care to everyone.

Learn about the latest on visitation guidelines

Mask policy

Main Line Health's commitment to patient and employee safety are essential priorities as we continue to provide exceptional care to the community. The Centers for Disease Control and Prevention emphasized that its new masking recommendations for people fully vaccinated against COVID-19 do not apply to health care settings. This means that all staff, patients, residents and visitors should continue to wear masks as recommended in all healthcare facilities. At Main Line Health, we are still requiring everyone to wear a mask on our campuses per the CDC’s guidance. As a healthcare provider, we believe mask wearing will keep all of our staff, patients, visitors and volunteers safe.
